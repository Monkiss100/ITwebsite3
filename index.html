Everyday we rely on informantion technology more and more. Because of this cyber security is a rising concern for many people and businesses. The majority of people are unaware of cyber security and don't know how to protect themselves online. Cyber security is always evolving and so are the tactics hackers use to try and get around it. Because of this is can be hard for the average person to keep up. This site will give someone who is new to the idea of cyber security a beginners guide to it. This site will cover the basics of cyber security, the different types of hackers and how to can prevent yourself from being a victim of a cyber attack.
