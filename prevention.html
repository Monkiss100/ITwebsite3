Prevention

For the average person there are many steps they can take to protect themsevles online. This is now important to keep your data safe and secure.

Passwords

Creating strong passwords is the most important thing to keep your accounts safe. This is because the most common way people steal accounts is from finding their password. Because of this it is also important to use different passwords for every account. Using a password manager can help with this because it creates completly random passwords and manages them for you.

Public Wifi

When using public wifi you should try to avoid typing any sensitive information. This could be account details like usernames or passwords and especially credit card information. This is because if you are on a shared network a skilled person could easily steal your data. On a shared network public network you should only use it to browse the internet.

Use a VPN

A VPN is a virtual private network. A VPN keeps your data hidden and protected from external access. Most VPN's work by encrypting your data like your IP and then sending it between private servers. This stops unauthorized people from viewing your information and increases your internet privacy. A VPN is good if you use websites that aren't secure or if you are using public wifi.

Antivirus Software

Antivirus Software works by detecting, quarantining and then deleting code that is malicious. Modern antivirus software updates itself to always have protection against the latest viruses. Setting up a firewall can be an alternative to using antivirus software. A firewall filters your internet traffic before it enters your computer to make sure it is safe.
